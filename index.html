FAQ

  • What interior design services does Homezo Interiors offer?

    Homezo Interiors specializes in end-to-end interior design solutions including modular kitchens, living room setups, false ceiling designs, bedroom makeovers, custom furniture, and space planning. Whether it's a new home or renovation, we bring functionality and aesthetics together.

  • How long does a typical interior project take?

    The timeline depends on the scope of work, but on average, a full home interior project takes 4 to 8 weeks from design finalization to execution. We ensure timely delivery without compromising on quality or detail.

  • Can I customize the designs as per my preferences?

    Absolutely! All our designs are 100% customizable. We collaborate with you to understand your style, needs, and budget to deliver interiors that reflect your personality and lifestyle.

  • Do you offer a warranty on your interior work?

    Yes, we offer up to 5 years of warranty on most of our modular work, including cabinets, wardrobes, and hardware. Quality and customer satisfaction are our top priorities.

  • Can you work within a specific budget range?

    Absolutely. We tailor our designs and material selections to match your budget, offering cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality and aesthetics.
