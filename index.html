FAQ

    We offer end-to-end interior design solutions including space planning, custom furniture, modular kitchens, décor, and complete home & office makeovers.

    The timeline depends on project size and scope. On average, home interiors take 6–10 weeks from planning to final installation.

    Yes, all our designs are tailor-made to suit your lifestyle, taste, and budget, ensuring every space feels unique and personal.
